Hello, my name is Jessica Molenaar. I am a Software/Front End Developer living in the Seattle area.

I am a self-motivated software engineer seeking a job as an Intern or Junior Developer with a fun and creative team. All my life I loved to tinker and figure out how things work (or why they don't). This curiosity coupled with passion for the amazing technologies that surround us every day brought me into the tech industry. After several years of self-guided learning, I enrolled in a fast-pace, insanely intense 14-week coding school where I received training in full-stack web development.

Why hire me?

#1) I am hardworking and motivated Enrolling in a coding school required the fortitude to drop everything and convince my families that what I'm doing isn’t crazy. Then I had to unlock the stamina and self-discipline to spend 70 to 90 hours a week coding – an equivalent of two full-time jobs.

#2) This isn't my first career In addition to an MBA, my 15 years of operations management and professional experience has taught me transferable skills such as teamwork, time management, and project management which are priceless in any career. I can round-out a team by bringing a unique combination of passion and fresh perspectives that you may not get from a recent college grad.

#3) Growth Investment As a former manager, I can’t imagine any better indicator of growth potential than someone who is passionate enough to put themselves and their livelihood on the line to prove that they have the aptitude to strive to be a successful programmer – with the right mentorship, of course. From my years of interviewing and hiring, I know that the qualified candidate is not always the best candidate. A good employee is passionate about their job, willing to learn and keep learning, and plays well with others.
